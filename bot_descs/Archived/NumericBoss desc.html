NumericBoss is a simple utility bot to have in your back pocket. It can do coin flips and dice rolls. But most importantly, it can calculate math problems. Simply pass an arithmetic expression into the calculator command and get your answer ASAP. It can do the basics like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, but it also calculates exponentiation, roots, logarithms, trigonometry, and some statistics. Typing =help is all you need to get started.

Here are the main commands:
Randomness
=flip or =coin Flip a two-sided coin
=roll or =dice Roll a six-sided die
=rand {Start} {End} Generate a random integer

The calculator
=c {Problem} or =calc {Problem} Calculate a math problem (examples below)

=c 100! 99! 98! 97! 96!
=c sin(cos(tan(asin(acos(atan(1))))))
=calc sqrt(i^123456789)
=calc abs(log(cbrt(-1)))
The grapher
=graph {Ordered pairs} Graph some ordered-pairs (example below)

=graph (0 ,2) , ( 3,4), (5, 12 )

Note the braces {} are only placeholders and should not be typed.
